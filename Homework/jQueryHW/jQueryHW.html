The BEST at Home Smoothie Bowl Recipe

Smoothie bowls are my go-to breakfast food but when I first started making them I struggled with knowing what to put in and how to make them etc. After making tons of smoothie bowls I have found the perfect combination to create the best smoothie bowl. This recipe is super easy, fast and delicious. Any of the ingerdients can be substituted for whatever you like, this is just what I prefer in my bowl! Hope you enjoy!

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • Helpful Tips

    Base:

  • 1/2 frozen banana
  • One banana (not frozen)
  • One cup frozen strawberries
  • 1/2 cup almond milk (or any liquid you prefer)

    • Toppings:*just suggestions*

  • Any fruits of choice (I like strawberry, banana, blueberry and kiwi)
  • Granola
  • Chia seeds
  • One tablesepoon peanut butter or almond butter (or both!)
  • Coconut flakes
  • Honey or agave
  1. Blend the bananas, strawberries and milk in a blender for 30 secondes- one minute
  2. Grab a bowl and place a little bit of granola at the bottom of it
  3. Pour the blended base into the bowl and out more granola on top
  4. Cut your fruit into bite size pieces and arrange it on top of the granola
  5. Sprinkle on any desiered seeds, cocnut flakes or additional toppings
  6. Add a tablespoon of peanut butter and enjoy!
  • Pulse your blender if you are having trouble blending. Do not add more liquid or else it will turn into a smoothie and not a bowl base
  • Store pre blended bases in the fridge if you are rushed in the mornings
  • Layer your fruit and granola with the base to get everything in one bite
  • Add some spinach or kale to the base to get your greens in for the day
