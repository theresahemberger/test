Best Sites to Book Accomodations:

  • AirBnB Great for people who don't want to stay in a hotel!
  • Hotels.com Easy and fast way to find cheap hotels
  • VRBO Also good for non-hotel lovers!

Best Sites to Book Flights:

  • Google Flights Easiest site to use to look for flights and immediately book as well!
  • Scott's Cheap Flights Requires you to sign up, but is is free!

Packing Tips:

  • Roll your clothes!
  • Only pack a carry on to save money
  • Wear your heaviest items on the flight. Ex- Boots, sweatshirts, jackets etc.
  • Bring simple clothing that matches everything
  • Bring mainly dark clothes and very few white items because they stain very easily
  • Only pack clothes you know you will actually wear
  • Leave room when packing to allow for shopping while on vacation!
