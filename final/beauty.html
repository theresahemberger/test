Ideal Skincare Routine

Everybody has different skin. My skin in pretty oily and I strggle with an uneven complextion and large pores. You might have dry skin or combination skin or anything else. Therefore I can not tell you exactly what products you have to use. However, the basic steps of a skin care routine are pretty similar for everyone no matter what your skin type is. I have listed below the order you should use your products in for best results. The can obviously be changed but these are just the basics.

    Morning

  1. Wash your face with a gentle cleanser
  2. Pat your face dry
  3. Use a toner
  4. Apply a serum
  5. Optional step: use a jade roller or rose quartz roller to get rid of morning puffiness (Link to Face Rollers)
  6. Allow a couple minutes to set the serum soak in
  7. Apply an eye cream. Tip: Use your ring finger to apply it and be gentle! No matter your age this is important!)
  8. Finally, apply sunscreen as moisturizer. So important!!!

    Night

  1. Wash your face with a gentle cleanser
  2. Exfoliate at night 2-3 times a week. This could be a product or using an exfoliator brush with your face wash
  3. Pat your face dry
  4. If you are doing a face mask, which I reccommend you do twice a week, put it on after washing you face
  5. Use a toner
  6. Use your serum and let it soak in
  7. Apply eye cream, use ring finger again
  8. Apply a thick moisturizer
