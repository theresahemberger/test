Outfit Inspo

We all know the feeling of being stressed out when trying to pick out an oufit. Whether it is before school, before a date or just for dinner with some friends. It can be extremely frustrating if nothing in your closet looks good that day and ypu just can't put together a cut outfit. It happens to all of us! I have constructed a guide for you guys for three different outfits so that next time you are stressing about what to wear it will hopefully be a little easier!

    First Outfit:

  1. Pick out your favorite favorite bottoms. It could be jeans, pants, shorts or a skirt
  2. Choose a simple white top. This could be a cropped tank top or t-shirt or a long sleeve. Whatever you like or have!
  3. Tuck the shirt into your jeans
  4. Take any belt you have and put it on
  5. Now throw on a cardigan if you want to be more casual or a leather jacket if you are going for a more edgy look
  6. Cuff your jeans and put on your favorite pair of booties
  7. Finish it off with some gold or siler jewlery and maybe some sunglasses

    Second Outfit: (probably the easiest)

  1. Pick out your favorite black dress. Any neutral color could work too, I just prefer black
  2. Throw a blazer over the dress. If you don't have ablazer you could wear something else but I highly suggest purchsing a blazer because it is a great staple intem to have in your closet
  3. Pick out your favorite white sneakers
  4. Finally add some jewlery

    Third Outfit: (the most casual)

  1. Throw on your comfiest black leggings
  2. Grab a comfy hoodie. I think this looks best with natural colors and also a cropped sweatshirt
  3. Throw a leather jacket over the hoodie
  4. Put on your favorite sneakers and you're ready to head out the door!
